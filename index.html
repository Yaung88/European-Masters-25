EUROPEAN MASTERS 2025

The European Star Wars Armada community is honored to invite you to the reactivated European Championship. The Championship will be held in a different location each year. The leaders of the European Community have chosen Poland, Gdańsk, as the location for the first tournament. The tournament will take place during the AleGramy festival organized on 15/16.11.2025.
