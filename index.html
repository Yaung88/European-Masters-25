EUROPEAN MASTERS 2025

The European Star Wars Armada community is honored to invite you to the reactivated European Championship. The Championship will be held in a different location each year. The leaders of the European Community have chosen Poland, Gdańsk, as the location for the first tournament. The tournament will take place during the AleGramy festival organized on 15/16.11.2025.

Meet the City – Gdańsk

Discover Gdańsk – a city where history meets the sea.

Located on the Baltic coast, Gdańsk is one of Poland’s most beautiful and historically rich cities. Walk through the colorful streets of the Old Town, visit the iconic Neptune Fountain, and admire the Gothic St. Mary's Church – one of the largest brick churches in the world. Don’t miss the World War II Museum and the European Solidarity Centre, both offering powerful insights into the city’s past. For a relaxing stroll, head to the Motława River promenade or take a short trip to the beach in nearby Brzeźno. Gdańsk is a perfect blend of culture, history, and seaside charm.
